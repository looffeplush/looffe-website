Welcome to Looffe

B-ear is a fully poseable little bear, available in two colors: black and white. Its nose matches the color too—the black version even has a completely transparent nose. To make it more poseable, we’ve embedded magnets in its feet, so it can stand securely on certain surfaces and pull off all kinds of tricky poses. On top of that, the bottom of B-ear’s feet can be customized with embroidered text, making each one truly unique.

We`ve also created a variety of outfits for B-ear, so it can be anything it wants to be.
